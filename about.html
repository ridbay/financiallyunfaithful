Hi, I'm Parthenia Walker!

I'm really happy you're here! I'm an accountant, and my specialty is figuring out smart ways for you to deal with taxes. On top of that, I'm super into teaching about personal finance, and I'm the big boss (CEO) at Financially Faithful. What I'm all about is making sure you do well not just in your business, but in every part of your life.

This all started when I was a kid, seeing my dad work really hard jobs for a long time. He did it all the way until he retired, but when he did, there wasn't much to show for all that effort. Looking back, I wish someone had been there to help him with our families money and taxes. So now, I want to be that guide for you and help you figure out the money and taxes side of your business, so you don't have to stress about it and can enjoy the rewards of your hard work.
